Stat Definitions:

  • H (hands): # of hands logged for user (sample size).
  • VPIP (Voluntarily Put In Pot): % of hands player has bet, raised or called preflop (does not include blinds because blinds are not voluntary).
  • PFR (Pre-Flop Raise): % of hands player has raised preflop.
  • AF (Aggression Factor): Unitless post-flop stat defined as (Bets + Raises) / Calls.
  • CB (Continuation Bet): % of hands player continuation bets the flop out of the hands player was the pre-flop aggressor.
  • 2B (Double Barrel): % of hands player double barrels on the turn out of the hands player Continuation Bet.
  • 3Ba (Triple Barrel): % of hands player triple barrels on the river out of the hands player double barreled.
  • 3B (3 Bet): % of hands player 3 bet pre-flop when given the opportunity.
  • 4B (4 Bet): % of hands player 4 bet pre-flop when given the opportunity.
  • FC (Fold to Continuation bet): % of hands player has folded when facing a continuation bet.
  • F2B (Fold to Double Barrel): % of hands player has folded when facing a double barrel.
  • F3Ba (Fold to Triple Barrel): % of hands player has folded when facing a triple barrel.
  • F3 (Fold to 3 bet): % of hands player has folded facing a 3 bet preflop.
  • WTSD (Went To Show Down): % of hands that player has gone to showdown with after seeing the flop.

By clicking a stat tile in the popup window it will toggle the label for the stat. So clicking [VPIP] will change it into [L:VPIP] and show "/VPIP: 12.3/" instead of "/ 12.3 /" on the overlay.

Stat Categories:

Note that stats for each user are separated into three categories:

  • Heads Up (2 players)
  • Short Handed (3-6 players)
  • Full Ring (7+ players)

For example, if I am playing short handed then only stats from hands that were also short handed will be shown.

Hand Histories:

Hand histories are gathered by default and can be accessed from the popup via the data tab. Because of chrome limitations on the amount of storage an extension can use, hand histories are capped at 2000 hands. Once this limit is reached you will be notified by an alert and hand histories will stop recording until the hand history is cleared (also via the data tab). The recording of hand histories can be turned on and off with the checkbox under (you guessed it) the data tab of the popup.

Note: a full hand history has no impact on stat gathering. Stats will continue to be gathered even when the hand history is full.
